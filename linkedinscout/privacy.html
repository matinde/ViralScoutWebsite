Privacy Policy for LinkedInScout

Last Updated:

Introduction

LinkedInScout ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

LinkedIn Post Data

  • We analyze LinkedIn posts that appear in your feed
  • We collect engagement metrics (likes, comments, shares) only from publicly visible LinkedIn posts
  • We do not collect personal information from LinkedIn posts or user profiles
  • All post analysis happens locally in your browser

License Information

  • Browser ID (randomly generated for license verification)
  • License key
  • License status and type
  • Installation timestamp
  • Number of remaining installations

Local Storage

  • History of viral posts you've encountered
  • Analytics data (number of posts analyzed, viral posts found)
  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • No browsing history or activity tracking outside of LinkedIn

Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to delete your data
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided above.

Changes to LinkedIn Posts

  • We do not modify or interact with LinkedIn posts
  • We only observe and analyze publicly available engagement metrics
  • All badges and indicators are displayed locally in your browser

Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
