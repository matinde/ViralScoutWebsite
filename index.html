Virality Metrics and Badges

The extensions will show a tooltip on all posts and indicate their levels of engagement.

Red Badge

This badge indicates that the content has gone viral, achieving exceptional engagement rates.

Orange Badge

This badge signifies that the content is currently trending and gaining traction.

Yellow Badge

This badge is awarded to content that is showing signs of growth and engagement.

Green Badge

This badge indicates that the content is showing signs of a growing engagement.

Gray Badge

This badge is for content that has a normal engagement rate, indicating normal level of interaction.
