Privacy Policy for TwitterScout Extension

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

TwitterScout ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 License Information

  • License key
  • Browser identifier (for license validation)
  • License status and type

2.2 Technical Data

  • Browser type and version
  • Installation timestamp
  • Extension usage statistics

2.3 Twitter/X Content Analysis

  • Public post metrics (likes, comments, retweets, views)
  • Post timestamps
  • Public engagement rates
  • Post content for viral potential analysis
  • Public account metrics visible on posts

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 License Management

  • Verify and validate your license
  • Ensure proper functionality of the extension
  • Prevent unauthorized use

3.2 Extension Functionality

  • Analyze Twitter/X posts for viral potential
  • Generate engagement metrics
  • Provide real-time notifications
  • Store viral post history locally

3.3 Service Improvement

  • Debug and optimize extension performance
  • Improve viral detection algorithms
  • Enhance user experience

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

  • Viral post history
  • Usage statistics
  • License information
  • Extension preferences

4.2 Server Storage

  • Only license keys and associated browser IDs
  • No personal user data is stored on our servers

5. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Track your browsing history
  • Store your Twitter/X login information
  • Access private Twitter/X data

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Secure HTTPS communication
  • Encrypted license verification
  • Local data storage encryption
  • Secure API endpoints

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored license information
  • Request deletion of your license data
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Uninstall the extension at any time

8. Changes to Posts

The extension:

  • Does not modify any Twitter/X content
  • Only adds visual indicators for viral potential
  • Does not interact with posts automatically

9. Data Retention

  • Local data: Stored until extension uninstall
  • License data: Retained while license is active
  • Usage statistics: Reset upon uninstallation

10. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for use by individuals under 13 years of age.

11. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes through the extension or our website.

Users can:

15. Termination
